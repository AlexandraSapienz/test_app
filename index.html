W 28–45, mid income

67.5% managerial/professional. Telegram as primary communication platform. Highest esoteric interest rate of any diaspora. Concentrated in NY, LA, NJ, CA.

Section 04

Monetisation ladder

Four revenue layers activated sequentially. Each unlocks after the previous one validates user willingness to engage and pay.

LayerTimingMechanicRevenue driver
Ads Day 1 Telegram native ads + AdSense in webview. Cap: 1 impression/session. $1.10–9 CPM by market. Cash-flow positive early.
One-time IAP Month 1 Full natal chart ($2.99), relationship compatibility ($3.99) Converts non-subscribers. Validates premium intent.
Freemium sub Month 1+ Weekly $1.99 · Monthly $6.99 · Annual $39.99 (USD). R$9–15 in Brazil. Core LTV. Annual plan is where margin lives.
AI chat readings Month 2+ Conversational follow-up questions on any card or horoscope. Session extension. Upsell trigger. Retention driver.
Freemium gate: Free = daily 1-card pull + basic sun-sign horoscope. Locked = 3-card spreads, birth chart, compatibility reports, weekly/monthly forecasts, AI chat. The gate hits after the user has already experienced value on day 1 — the 3-card spread is the perfect unlock trigger.
Brazil payments: Launch with Telegram Payments 2.0 (Stripe). Add Pix via Mercado Pago or PagSeguro in month 2. Pix is how Brazil pays — skipping it caps conversion at 40–60% of potential.
